Privacy Policy

SA Connect NZ

Last updated: February 2026

SA Connect NZ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely using cryptographic hashing — we never store your password in plain text)
  • Family name

Profile Information

To help you connect with other South African families in New Zealand, you may provide:

  • A short bio about your family
  • Suburb and/or city
  • GPS coordinates (used to show nearby families)
  • Interests and hobbies
  • Family member first names and ages
  • Profile photo

User-Generated Content

We collect content you create within the app, including:

  • Messages sent to other families
  • Events you create or RSVP to
  • Business listings you submit to the Business Hub

Uploaded Media

Profile photos and business listing images are uploaded and stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your family profile to other registered users
  • Enable location-based discovery of nearby families
  • Facilitate private messaging between families
  • Display community events and business listings
  • Send password reset emails when requested
  • Process subscription payments
  • Improve and maintain the app

We do not use your information for third-party advertising, behavioural tracking, or selling to data brokers.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Replit's infrastructure. Uploaded images (such as profile photos and business logos) are stored on our server.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard cryptographic algorithms before storage
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Access controls to limit who can access stored data

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services to operate SA Connect NZ:

Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)

We use Brevo to send transactional emails, specifically password reset emails. When you request a password reset, your email address is shared with Brevo for the purpose of delivering that email. Brevo's privacy policy can be found on their website.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription payments. Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play, and RevenueCat helps us verify and manage your subscription status. Payment details (such as credit card numbers) are handled entirely by Apple or Google — we never see or store your payment information. RevenueCat's privacy policy can be found on their website.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With other app users: Your profile information (family name, bio, suburb/city, interests, family members, and profile photo) is visible to other registered users of the app to facilitate connections
  • With service providers: As described in Section 4, we share limited data with Brevo (email delivery) and RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • As required by law: We may disclose your information if required to do so by New Zealand law or in response to valid legal requests

Your GPS coordinates are used to calculate approximate distances but are not displayed directly to other users.

6. Children's Privacy

SA Connect NZ is designed for families and is intended for use by adults (18 years and older). While family profiles may include first names and ages of children, accounts can only be created and managed by adults.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • View your data: You can view all information associated with your account within the app
  • Edit your data: You can update your profile information, family members, interests, and other details at any time through the app
  • Delete your account: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Settings screen within the app. This action is irreversible and removes your profile, messages, events, and business listings

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you also have the right to request access to and correction of your personal information. To exercise these rights, you may contact us at the email address below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our systems, including:

  • Your profile information and family details
  • Your messages
  • Events you have created
  • Business listings you have submitted
  • Uploaded images (profile photos and business logos)

Some anonymised or aggregated data may be retained for operational purposes (e.g., total user count), but this data cannot be used to identify you.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:
