Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a family profile?
Sign up, then follow the prompts to add your family name, members, interests, and location.
How do I connect with other families?
Browse the Discover tab to find families nearby. Tap "Connect" to send a connection request.
How do I message another family?
Once connected, you can send messages through the Messages tab.
How do I create or join an event?
Go to the Events tab to browse community events or create your own.
How do I list my business?
Visit the Business Hub tab and tap "Add Business" to create a free listing.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data.
How do I reset my password?
On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot Password" and follow the email verification steps.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Passwords are encrypted, and your data is stored securely. See our Privacy Policy for details.
